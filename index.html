Day 1: Circular Flow Vocabulary

Review these key terms before starting today's activities. Knowing these definitions will help you answer the checkpoint questions easily!

Households: Individuals or groups living together who own resources and buy goods.
Businesses: Firms that buy resources to produce and sell goods and services.
Product Market: The place where businesses sell finished goods and services to households.
Resource Market: The place where households sell resources (like labor) to businesses.
Circular Flow: A model showing how money, goods, and resources move through the economy.
